# Siraya glossing markers — surface affix forms (alphabetical)

All markers from Adelaar (2011), sorted alphabetically by marker. Form-type key: `PREFIX-` · `-SUFFIX` / `=CLITIC` · `‹INFIX›` / `rdp` · `CIRCUM-…-FIX` · `free` (free morpheme / zero / pronoun form)

---

| Marker | Meaning | Category | Surface forms | Notes |
|--------|---------|----------|---------------|-------|
| `1pe` | 1st pl exclusive | Person & number | `free ĭmian` (indep) · `=CLITIC =kame` (nom) · `=CLITIC =(m)ian / =(m)iän` (gen) · `free ĭmian-än` (obl) | Excludes the addressee; independent form *ĭmian*; `=kame` is the nominative clitic |
| `1pi` | 1st pl inclusive | Person & number | `free ĭmĭta` (indep) · `=CLITIC =kĭta` (nom) · `=CLITIC =eta / =mĭta` (gen) · `free ĭmitä-n` (obl) | Includes the addressee; independent form *ĭmĭta* |
| `1s` | 1st sg | Person & number | `free ĭau` (indep) · `=CLITIC =ko` (nom) · `=CLITIC =(m)au` (gen) · `free ĭau-an` (obl) | Independent form *ĭau*; `=ko` is the nominative clitic |
| `2p` | 2nd pl | Person & number | `free ĭmumi` (indep) · `=CLITIC =kamu` (nom) · `=CLITIC =(m)umi` (gen) · `free ĭmumi-än` (obl) | Independent form *ĭmumi* |
| `2s` | 2nd sg | Person & number | `free ĭmhu` (indep) · `=CLITIC =kaw` (nom) · `=CLITIC =oho / =(m)hu` (gen) · `free ĭmhu-an` (obl) | Independent form *ĭmhu* |
| `3p` | 3rd pl | Person & number | `free neini` (indep) · `free ta neini` (nom) · `free nein` (gen) · `free neini-än` (obl) | Independent form *neini*; 3rd person pronouns are free forms, not clitics |
| `3s` | 3rd sg | Person & number | `free teni` (indep) · `free ta teni` (nom) · `free tin` (gen) · `free tini-än` (obl) | Independent form *teni*; 3s pronoun `tini-än` is the most frequent oblique form |
| `add` | additive ("and, also, too") | Discourse & clause | `=CLITIC =ăpa` · `=CLITIC ='pa` | `='pa` is short form after final vowel; *l* inserted before `=ato` and `=ăpa` on subjunctive suffix (e.g. `i-rua-al=ăpa`); `ăsi=ăpa` = "not even" |
| `adv` | adversative ("but, however, contrast") | Discourse & clause | `free nda` · `=CLITIC =da` · `=CLITIC =ra` | `nda` is free form; `=da` and `=ra` are enclitic variants; can attach to verbs, nouns, or entire clauses; also marks introduction of new information |
| `ao` | actor-oriented (general) | Voice | `PREFIX- m-` · `PREFIX- ma-` · `‹INF› ‹m›` · `free ∅` | ∅ (zero) for class 2 verbs; `m-` for class 3; `ma-` for classes 1 and 4; `‹m›` infix after initial consonant of class 3 roots (e.g. `k‹m›ĭta`). `{m}` notation covers all three |
| `ao1` | actor-oriented, class 1 verbs | Voice | `PREFIX- ma-` · `PREFIX- ka-` | `ma-` is the actor-oriented form; `ka-` is the underlying class-forming prefix (appears in uo forms, nominals, and after `pa-`) |
| `ao3` | actor-oriented, class 3 verbs | Voice | `‹INF› ‹m›` | Infixed after initial consonant; realised as `hm-` when root begins with *h* (e.g. *hmamia*); absent in some subjunctive actor-oriented forms |
| `ao4` | actor-oriented, class 4 verbs | Voice | `PREFIX- ma-` · `PREFIX- maku-` · `PREFIX- mătäi-` · `PREFIX- maki-` · `PREFIX- paki-` | Class 4 verbs are compound/derived; ao marker varies by bound verb; `pa-` class-forming prefix appears in uo and nominal forms |
| `as` | anticipating sequence | Derivational | `free CV(C)- (copied)` | First syllable(s) of lexical verb prefixed to auxiliary; not a morpheme but a copying process; e.g. `kmi-` copied from `k‹m›ĭta` onto auxiliary `kmi-dŭng` |
| `caus` | causative | Derivational | `PREFIX- pa-` · `PREFIX- pa-ka-` · `PREFIX- pa-pa-` · `PREFIX- p-` | `pa-` (cl. 2 & 3 directly to root); `pa-ka-` (cl. 1, combining with `ka-`); `pa-pa-` (cl. 4); shortened to `p-` before orientation prefixes `u-`/`äw-` (e.g. `p-u-pänäx`) |
| `coll` | collective (species/class/group) | Case & nominal | `-SUFFIX -an` | Suffixed to noun roots; generic plural referring to a class or species; e.g. `kaäwlung-an` 'humanity', `ăsu-ăsu-an` 'dogs (in general)' |
| `com` | comitative orientation ("with, together") | Derivational | `PREFIX- a-` · `PREFIX- ä-` | `ä-` is palatalised variant; e.g. `a-para` 'to take along', `a-kua` 'to obey'. Few attested derivations |
| `df` | default case marker | Case & nominal | `free ki` · `free k'=` | `ki` before common nouns as undergoer (ao clause) or possessor; reduced to `k'=` before vowel-initial words |
| `dst` | distal | Deixis | `free ăna` · `free hĭna` · `free k'=ăna` | `ăna` = "that (distal), then"; `hĭna` = "there"; `k'=ăna` = "that one (distal, already mentioned)" |
| `gen` | genitive (actor not in subject; possessor) | Case & nominal | `free ki` · `=CLITIC =(m)au` · `=CLITIC =oho / =(m)hu` · `free tin` · `=CLITIC =eta / =mĭta` · `=CLITIC =(m)ian` · `=CLITIC =(m)umi` · `free nein` | `ki` = default genitive marker for common nouns; clitic forms for pronouns: `=(m)au` (1s), `=oho`/`=(m)hu` (2s), `tin` (3s), `=eta`/`=mĭta` (1pi), `=(m)ian` (1pe), `=(m)umi` (2p), `nein` (3p) |
| `imp` | imperative | Mood | `-SUFFIX -i` · `-SUFFIX -u` · `-SUFFIX -a` · `-SUFFIX -ey` · `-SUFFIX -aw` | `-i` and `-u` are exclusively imperative suffixes; subjunctive forms `-a` (ao), `-ey`/`-aw` (uo) also express commands (more common than `-i`/`-u`) |
| `incl` | inclusive (totality circumfix) | Case & nominal | `CIRCUM- ka- …-an` · `CIRCUM- ka- …-ang` | Circumfix; "all of X, entire X"; e.g. `ka-tălax-an` 'entire household'; also nominalises class 1 verbs: `ka-patey-an` 'death', `ka-irang-ǝn` 'goodness' |
| `inch` | inchoative ("become X") | Derivational | `PREFIX- mi-` · `PREFIX- käwx-` | `mi-` prefixed to class 1 verbs; `käwx-` prefixed to class 1 verbs and nouns. Can combine with causative: `pa-käwx-` |
| `inv` | inversive ("undo, reverse") | Derivational | `PREFIX- aäw-` · `PREFIX- ääw-` | Variant spellings of same prefix; adds meaning of 'un-, dis-, reverse'; e.g. `m-aäw-ka-kirix` 'to untie' |
| `lk` | linker | Discourse & clause | `free ka` · `free k'=` | `ka` co-ordinates verb phrases; introduces complement, causal, and relative clauses; reduced to `k'=` before vowel; also links numeral + noun |
| `loc` | location orientation ("in space/time") | Derivational | `PREFIX- i-` · `PREFIX- ĭ-` | `ĭ-` is the reduced variant; semantically bleached in many derivations; e.g. `m-i-rung` 'to sit', `i-rua` 'to arrive' |
| `mot` | motion orientation ("toward/into") | Derivational | `PREFIX- u-` · `PREFIX- äw-` | `äw-` is the palatalised variant; often combines with causative `p(a)-u-` or `p(a)-äw-`; e.g. `m-u-rbo` 'go inside', `m-äw-äwma` 'go to city' |
| `neg` | negator | Discourse & clause | `free ăsi` · `free ĭna` · `free ra` | `ăsi` is the main negator; takes pronominal clitics like an auxiliary; `ĭna` used with 1pe (`ĭna=kame`); `ra`/`nda` is the adversative/mild negator ("not; but not") |
| `nom` | nominative (subject marker) | Case & nominal | `free ta` · `free ti` | `ta` precedes common-noun subjects; `ti` is the personal article (precedes human proper names); nominative pronouns are cliticised (`=ko`, `=kaw`, `=kame`, `=kamu`, `=kĭta`, `ta teni`, `ta neini`) |
| `obl` | oblique (all other roles) | Case & nominal | `-SUFFIX -än` · `free ĭau-an` · `free ĭmhu-an` · `free tini-än` · `free ĭmitä-n` · `free ĭmian-än` · `free ĭmumi-än` · `free neini-än` | `-än` suffix on human nouns; free oblique pronoun forms listed; 3s pronoun `tini-än` is the most frequent |
| `pa` | personal article | Case & nominal | `free ti` | Precedes human proper names in all syntactic positions; e.g. `ti Jesus`, `ti Joannes-an` |
| `poss` | possessive | Case & nominal | `free a=` | Possessive clitic; e.g. `a=muhu` 'yours (2s)'; often fused with following word |
| `prf` | perfective | Tense & aspect | `-SUFFIX =ato` · `-SUFFIX ='to` | Enclitic; short form `='to` after final vowel/diphthong; `=atu=` before further clitics; attaches after uo/sj suffix, before pronominal clitics |
| `prx` | proximal | Deixis | `free ăta` · `free hia` · `free k'=ăta` | `ăta` = deictic noun "this one, this person"; `hia` = "here"; `k'=ăta` = "these (stones)" |
| `pst` | past tense | Tense & aspect | `PREFIX- ni-` | Prefixed to verb; absent in subordinate clauses introduced by *ĭru*/*ru*/*du* and in infinitives within complex verb phrases |
| `qu` | question word | Discourse & clause | `free kawa` · `free kimang` | `kawa` = "what, which; is it that?"; `kimang` = "what?"; free morphemes introducing interrogative clauses |
| `rdp` | reduplication | Tense & aspect | `‹INF› Ca-` · `free root-rdp` | `Ca-` copies first C of root + *a*: progressive aspect or stative on verbs; deverbal nouns (agent/instrument/undergoer). Full root reduplication on nouns = plurality/variety/indefiniteness |
| `sj` | subjunctive (actor-oriented) | Mood | `-SUFFIX -a` · `-SUFFIX -al=` | `-a` is the actor-oriented subjunctive suffix; surfaces as `-al-` before clitics `=ato` and `=ăpa` (e.g. `i-rua-al=ăpa-ko`). Also used for imperative |
| `sj.uo` | subjunctive + undergoer (portmanteau) | Mood | `-SUFFIX -ey` · `-SUFFIX -eyl=` · `-SUFFIX -aw` · `-SUFFIX -awl=` · `-SUFFIX -aney` · `-SUFFIX -aneyl=` | `-ey`/`-aw`/`-aney` before clitics become `-eyl-`/`-awl-`/`-aneyl-` (*l* inserted at morpheme boundary before `=ato`, `=ăpa`). `-ey` is the dominant/default form; `-aw` is lexically restricted; `-aney` highlights non-core/locative argument |
| `st` | stative | Voice | `PREFIX- ma-` | Class 1 verbs with `ma-` in actor position express a state or property (e.g. `ma-riang` 'be big/good'). No separate stative morpheme; function is conflated with `ao1` |
| `uo` | undergoer-oriented (indicative) | Voice | `-SUFFIX -ǝn` · `-SUFFIX -n` · `-SUFFIX -an` · `-SUFFIX -än` · `free ∅` | `-ǝn` is the default; `-n` after roots ending in vowel; `-an`/`-än` often locative or non-core argument; ∅ (zero) also attested in class 2. Neutralised to `-n` after roots ending in `-a` |
| `v1` | class 1 verb marker | Verb class | `PREFIX- ka-` | Underlying class prefix; appears overtly in uo forms, nominals, and in the causative combination `pa-ka-` |
| `v4` | class 4 verb marker | Verb class | `PREFIX- pa-` · `PREFIX- ma-` | `pa-` is the class-forming prefix in uo/nominal forms; `ma-` is the ao form. Class 4 causative yields `pa-pa-` |
