# Siraya free-form grammatical words — alphabetical reference

All free-form grammatical words from Adelaar (2011), sorted alphabetically by form. Category key at the end.

Category markers: **TENSE** · **MOOD** · **COND** · **VOICE** · **CASE** · **DISC** · **DEIXIS** · **QUANT** · **Q-WORD** · **INDEF**

---

| Form | Gloss | Category | Function | Notes & Siraya examples |
|------|-------|----------|----------|------------------------|
| `a` / `a=` | possessive preposition | **CASE** | "belonging to; from; about, concerning" | Introduces possessor; 3rd person and 1s take independent form after `a`; other pronouns are genitive clitics. *a=mhu-a ta sa-saat* (poss=2s.gen-sj nom rdp-one) "one is yours" (xx:14) |
| `akumea` / `aku-` | positive existential | **DISC** | "to exist; there is/are; to have" | Main existential verb; `aku-` is a bound form that incorporates its subject; also expresses possession. *akume'-a ta teni ki ka-huvar-an* (exist-sj nom 3s.nom df v1-abundant-uo) "he will have in abundance" (xiii:12); *ni-aku-yuax ki ma-dax ki vavuy* (pst-exist-herd df ao1-many df pig) "there was a herd of many swine" (ix:30) |
| `aley` | reason preposition | **CASE** | "because (of); on behalf of; in order to" | *aley ka* + predicate = "because / in order to"; *aley ki* + NP = "because of". *aley ka kiĭm-ǝn ta ma-mang k'=ăta ki Heidang* (reason lk seek-uo nom rdp-what lk=prox df Heathen) "because the Heathens seek after all these things" (vi:32) |
| `ăna` | distal | **DEIXIS** | "that one; that; then" | Deictic noun indicating distance; `k'=ăna` = "that (attributive)"; `ăna ta` = "then" (sequential). *teni ta ti ăna, t‹m›urung-a tini-än* (3s.nom nom pa dist ‹ao3›grab-sj 3s-obl) "that's the one, grab him!" (xxvi:48); *äwma k'=ăna* "that village" (xxi:2) |
| `ăna ta` | temporal/sequential connective | **TENSE** | "then, consequently" | Deictic distal *ăna* + nominative `ta`; used as sequence-marker between clauses. *ăna ta ni-ătaral tĭni-än ta lĭtu* (then pst-leave 3s-obl nom devil) "then the devil left him" (iv:11); *(ăna ta) kalang-aw=mumi tu vua nein* (then know-sj.uo=2p.gen loc fruit 3p.gen) "you shall know them by their fruits" (vii:20) |
| `ano` | indefinite pronoun | **INDEF** | "someone, so-and-so" | Indefinite human pronoun; always preceded by `ti`. *ti ano* "a certain person, so-and-so"; attested 2× in the Gospel (xii:47, xiii:19) as a placeholder for an unnamed human referent |
| `ăsi` | declarative negator | **DISC** | "no, not" | Main declarative negator; takes pronominal clitics like an auxiliary; `ăsi=ăpa` = "not yet / not even"; `ăsi-a` = "lest". *ăsi=kame hia ki mamang* (neg=1pe.nom here df whatever) "we do not have anything here" (xiv:17); *ăsi=kamu lava ni-k‹m›ŭtkŭt tu Ta-tuko* (neg=2p.nom perhaps pst-‹ao3›read loc Law) "have you not read in the Law?" (xii:5) |
| `ăta` | proximal | **DEIXIS** | "this one; this" | Deictic noun indicating proximity; `k'=ăta` = "this (attributive)". *ăta ta vual=au* (prox nom body=1s.gen) "this is my body" (xxvi:26); *wäi k'=ăta* "this day" (vi:11) |
| `ausi` | negative existential | **DISC** | "not exist, to be absent; to lack" | Negative counterpart of `akumea`; *ausi=ăpa* = "not yet, before"; *ausi ki* = "other than, except". *ausi ta teni hia* (not.exist nom 3s.nom here) "he is not here" (xxviii:6); *ausi ki mamang ta Alak ki aäwlung* (not.exist df whatever nom Child df person) "the Son of Man has nothing" (viii:20) |
| `du` | temporal/conditional conjunction | **COND** | "when; if" | Variant of `ĭru`; verbs in these clauses usually unmarked for past tense even in past narrative. *du ăsi=ăpa ni-hi-icip-ǝn* (when not=add pst-circumcise-uo) "when he was not yet circumcised" (C81) |
| `dumiaka` | consequential conjunction | **COND** | "so, then, consequently, in that way" | Clause-initial or mid-clause; marks logical consequence or result; apparently < *\*du mia ka*. *ru su-dĭk-ǝn=au s‹m›u-kla ki kulamux tĭn, dumiaka 'd-därikäx-a=ko* (if as-only-uo=1s.gen ‹ao3›touch-join df garment 3s.gen, then rdp-healthy-sj=1s.nom) "if I could only touch his garment, I will be healed" (ix:21) |
| `heyru` | temporal/conditional conjunction | **COND** | "when, at the time; if" | Variant of `ĭru`; *ĭru=ăpa ka* = "nevertheless" (C134v). *nda heyru* … *ka ni-pa-i-alak ta ti Jesus tu Bethlehem* (adv when,if lk pst-v4-loc-offspring nom pa Jesus loc Bethlehem) "when Jesus had been born in Bethlehem" (ii:1) |
| `hia` | locative adverb | **DEIXIS** | "here" | Deictic adverb matching `ăta`; *kuma hĭna* = "like that, thus". *m-i-e-rung-a hia* (ao-rdp-loc-sit-sj here) "sit here" (xxvi:36); *ausi ta teni hia* "he is not here" (xxviii:6) |
| `hĭna` | locative adverb | **DEIXIS** | "there" | Deictic adverb matching `ăna`; *kuma hĭna* = "like that, thus, as follows". *hĭna mako-Ali-lid* (there v4.invoke-rdp-God) "go there to pray" (xxvi:36); *kuma hĭna ki su* (like-there df word) "saying, as follows" |
| `ĭmid` | universal quantifier | **QUANT** | "all, everything, everyone" | Also takes suffix `-eyaw` for emphatic all-inclusiveness. *ĭmid ki ka-ilx-ǝn tu tunun ki vŭlŭm ki Näi* (all df v1-strong-uo loc heaven df earth) "all power in heaven and earth" (xxviii:18); *ĭmid ki ma-mang ni-pää-tunun ĭau-an ki Rama=au* "all these things were delivered to me by my Father" (xi:27) |
| `ĭna` | prohibitive negator | **MOOD** · **DISC** | "don't!" | Dedicated negative imperative; contrasts with `ăsi` (declarative negator). *ĭna ma-takut* (don't ao1-afraid) "don't be afraid" (xiv:27); *ĭna päx-kbu ki rĭx* (don't think-heart df mind) "don't worry" (vi:31) |
| `ĭru` / `ru` | temporal/conditional conjunction | **COND** | "when; if" | Core form of the conditional/temporal conjunction; variants `ru`, `du`, `ndu`, `heyru`. *Ĭru ka k‹m›ĭta tĭni-än, ni-mako-Ali-lid tĭni-än ta neni* (when,if lk ‹ao3›see 3s-obl pst-ao4.invoke-rdp-God 3s-obl nom 3p.nom) "when they saw him, they worshipped him" (xxviii:17); *ru kĭta-n nein ta nĭnux, uhang ni-ma-takut* (when,if see-uo 3p.gen nom earthquake great pst-ao1-fear) "when they saw the earthquake, they were very afraid" (xxvii:54) |
| `ka` / `k'=` | linker | **DISC** | co-ordinates VPs; introduces complement, causal, and relative clauses | The primary clause-level connective; `k'=` before vowel. *ka ăsi t'la-n ta äwĭng ka pa-itu-rbo-eyl=ato ki sato* (and neg light-uo nom candle and caus-be.at-go.inside-sj=prf df bushel) "neither do men light a candle and put it under a bushel" (v:15) |
| `kaumang` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "why?" | Causal question word; lit. "for/to what". *kaumang ka päx-kbu-n=umi päx-dimdim ki pa-mămi-an* (why lk as-heart-uo=2p.gen think-dark df v4-dress-uo) "why do you worry about clothing?" (vi:28) |
| `kidi` | modal auxiliary | **MOOD** | "ought to, should, must" | Free verb/auxiliary; takes clitics like a verb; *kidi=ato* "it behooves, must". *kidi=ato ka pa-vut-aw ta ma-mang k'=ăta* (ought.to=prf lk caus-happen-sj.uo nom rdp-what lk=prox) "all these things must come to pass" (xxiv:6) |
| `ki` | default case marker | **VOICE** · **CASE** | marks genitive actor / undergoer / possessor | Default non-subject marker for common nouns; reduced to `k'=` before vowel-initial words. *ni-p'xä-'n=ko ĭmid ki ka-ilx-ǝn* (pst-give-uo=1s.nom all df v1-strong-uo) "I was given all power" (xxviii:18) |
| `lam` | comitative preposition | **CASE** | "with, together with" | Followed by NP in default case. *ni-paki-valey nein ta rawey lam ki Rarenan tĭn* (pst-v4.seek-find 3p.gen nom child with df Mother 3s.gen) "they found the child with its Mother" (ii:11) |
| `lava` | epistemic adverb | **MOOD** | "possibly, maybe, perhaps" | Expresses speaker uncertainty; often in questions. *pa-i-alak-aw lava tumang ta ti Jesus* (v4-loc-give.birth-sj.uo perhaps where nom pa Jesus) "where will this Jesus be born?" (ii:4); *ăsi=mumi lava ni-k'tŭng* (neg=2p.gen maybe pst-...) "have you perhaps not understood?" (xvi:9) |
| `ma-batung` | quantity word | **QUANT** | "many (of people)" | Derives from *vatung/batung* "multitude of people". *mabatoung* (iv:25) "numerous (people)"; *ta ma-batung ki tau* "many people" |
| `ma-dax` | quantity word | **QUANT** | "many" | Derives from *dax* "multitude". *ni-aku-yuax ki ma-dax ki vavuy* (pst-exist-herd df ao1-many df pig) "there was a herd of many swine" (ix:30) |
| `ma-titing` | quantity word | **QUANT** | "few" | Derives from *-titing*. *ma-titing ki tau* "a few people"; contrasts with `ma-dax` "many" |
| `mamang` | indefinite pronoun | **INDEF** | "whatever, anything" | *mama* + *mang*; non-human indefinite. *ĭmid ki ma-mang ni-pää-tunun ĭau-an ki Rama=au* (all df rdp-what pst-give-pass.on 1s-obl df Father=1s.gen) "all these things were delivered to me by my Father" (xi:27) |
| `mama (ki) mang` / `mama imang` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "how?" | Lit. "like what"; comparative *mama* + *mang*. *kaumang ka päx-kbu-n=umi päx-dimdim* "why do you worry?" (vi:28); *mama imang ta* "how is it that..." (vi:28) |
| `mang` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "what?" | Root interrogative; appears in all compound question words. *mang ta kan-aw-miän* (what nom eat-sj.uo=1pe.gen) "what are we going to eat?" (vi:31) |
| `măka-` | ablative preposition/prefix | **CASE** | "from, since, for" | Usually attached to following word (borderline prefix). *măka-rbo* (ao4.come.from-inside) "from inside" (viii:28); *măka-saat ki wäi* (ao4.come.from-one df day) "all day long" (xx:6) |
| `măka-kuna` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "wherefrom?" | Ablative *măka-* + *kuna*. *măka-kuna* (xiii:56) "wherefrom?"; used to ask the source/origin of something or someone |
| `măno` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "when?" | Temporal question word; *măno-no* = "whenever". *măno ka ni-kĭta-n=ian ĭmhu-an ka ma-xangey* (when lk pst-see-uo=1pe.gen 2s-obl lk ao1-hungry) "when have we seen you hungry?" (xxv:44) |
| `măno-no` | indefinite pronoun | **INDEF** | "whenever" | Reduplicated temporal interrogative. *măno-no* (xxvi:16) "whenever"; reduplication of *măno* generalises the time reference from specific to indefinite |
| `misĭng` | assertive particle | **MOOD** | "truly, certainly, surely" | Affirmative particle; also written *mĭsĭng*. *Mĭsing ĭmhu ta na lam=ăpa nein* (true 2s.nom nom part with=add 3p.gen) "truly you also were with them" (xxvi:73); *misĭng kun=au=kamu=ra* "for sure I'm telling you" (vi:2) |
| `m-u-kuna` | concessive conjunction | **COND** | "although, in spite of" | Concessive; built on bound verb *kuna*. *m-u-kuna ka Tama-tna-m'sing, ka ăsi Tama-tna-m'sing* (although lk person-believe, lk neg person-believe) "believers as well as non-believers" (C101); *m-u-kuna ka-ĭnax-ǝn nein* "in spite of their reluctance" (C66v) |
| `na` | partitive/source preposition | **CASE** | "being part of; from; belonging to" | Marks partitive (part of a whole), source, or temporal starting point. *na alak ti David* (part offspring pa David) "a son of David's" (xxii:42); *na hnĭn-an k'=ăta* (part now-obl lk=prox) "from now onwards" (xxvi:64) |
| `na hnĭn-an` | temporal phrase | **TENSE** | "from now on" | Lit. "from now-obl"; *na* (partitive/source) + *hnĭn-an* (now-obl). *na hnĭn-an k'=ăta* (part now-obl lk=prox) "from now onwards" (xxvi:64) |
| `ndu` | temporal/conditional conjunction | **COND** | "when; if" | Variant of `ĭru`; cross-referenced in lexicon with `ĭru`, `du`, `heyru`, `ru`. *ndu* attested in Catechism; introduces same conditional/temporal clauses as `ĭru`; verbs typically unmarked for past |
| `pĭ-pĭna` / `pa-pĭna` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "how much/many?" | Reduplicated; Ca-rdp with human referents, first-syllable rdp with non-human. *pĭ-pĭna ki lbäx* (rdp-how.much df part) "how many parts?" (C6v); *Pĭ-pĭna='to ta paul ĭmumi-än* (rdp-how.much=prf nom bread 2p-obl) "how many breads do you have?" (xv:34) |
| `pulilit` | attention particle | **MOOD** | "take heed, pay attention, observe" | Imperative-like particle; directs attention; also glossed 'observe, notice'. *pulilit-aw ta seyluf ka tu puläx* (pay.attention-sj.uo nom lily lk loc field) "consider the lilies of the field" (vi:28); *pulilit* (take-heed) "seeing you will see, and will not perceive" (xiii:14) |
| `ra` / `nda` | adversative free form | **DISC** | "but, however; not" | Free forms of the adversative clitic `=da`/`=ra`; also introduces new information. *iru ka-kĭta-n tĭn ta vare ka ma-lix=da, ma-takut=ato* (when,if rdp-see-uo 3s.gen nom wind lk ao1-strong=adv, ao1-fear=prf) "but when he saw the strong wind, he became afraid" (xiv:30) |
| `ru` | temporal/conditional conjunction | **COND** | "when; if" | Variant of `ĭru`; see `ĭru` entry. *ru kĭta-n nein ta nĭnux, uhang ni-ma-takut* (when,if see-uo 3p.gen nom earthquake great pst-ao1-fear) "when they saw the earthquake, they were very afraid" (xxvii:54) |
| `ruma` | indefinite quantifier | **QUANT** | "some; other; another" | Non-human referent unreduplicated; human referent with Ca-reduplication. *vato ka ruma* (stone df other) "other stones" (xxii:2); *tau ka ra-ruma* (person df Ca-rdp-other) "other people" (xix:19) |
| `sa-saat` | definite quantifier | **QUANT** | "one; a single" | Numeral "one" used as indefinite article or with inclusive circumfix for totality. *sa-saat neini-än ka tu-lam ti Jesus-an* (rdp-one 3p-obl lk loc-be.with pa Jesus-obl) "one of those who were with Jesus" (xxvi:51); *saat ka-tălax-an* "the entire household" |
| `ta` | nominative marker | **VOICE** · **CASE** | marks subject noun phrase | Free case marker preceding common-noun subjects; *ta ti* before personal names. *akume'-a ta teni ki ka-huvar-an* "he will have in abundance" (xiii:12); *ni-aku-yuax ki ma-dax ki vavuy* — subject *ta vavuy* "swine" introduced by *ta* (ix:30) |
| `ti` | personal article | **CASE** | introduces human NPs in all cases | Precedes personal names, kinship terms *ina/ama*, pronouns *ĭau*, indefinite *ano*, deictic pronouns, ordinal numbers, and deverbal nouns with human reference. *teni ta ti ăna, t‹m›urung-a tini-än* (3s.nom nom pa dist ‹ao3›grab-sj 3s-obl) "that's the one, grab him!" (xxvi:48); *ti ĭau ta Alid ti Abraham* (pa 1s.indep nom God pa Abraham) "I am the God of Abraham" (xxii:32) |
| `timamang` | indefinite pronoun | **INDEF** | "whoever" | *ti* + *mamang*; human indefinite. *timamang ta t‹m›umimia, m-ara ta teni* (whoever nom ‹ao3›beg, ao3-receive nom 3s.nom) "whoever asks, will receive" (vii:8); *timamang=ăpa ta pa-tawrŭma-al=ato ki văti tĭn* "anyone who loses his soul" (xxv:29) |
| `timang` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "who?" | *ti* (personal article) + *mang*; human referent. *timang ta m-ara tĭni-än ki su k'=ăta* (who nom ao3-take 3s-obl df word lk=prox) "who is it that said this?"; attested at iii:7 as a direct question |
| `tu` | locative case marker | **CASE** | "to, in, at, on" (location/direction) | Locative case marker; also marks temporal location; forms compound prepositions with deictic verbs. *ni-taw-kua ta ti Jesus ki na da-diri* (pst-go.down/through nom pa Jesus df part rdp-sow) "Jesus went through the corn" (xii:1); *tu tunun ki vŭlŭm ki Näi* "in heaven and earth" (xxviii:18) |
| `tu äwx` / `tu äux` | temporal phrase | **TENSE** | "after" | Lit. "at the back/behind"; `tu` (locative) + noun *äwx* "position behind". *tu äux* (loc behind) "after" (i:12); *tu äwx ki pa-kakĭtil-an* "after the wedding" |
| `tumamang` | indefinite pronoun | **INDEF** | "wherever" | *tu* + *mamang*; locative indefinite. *tumamang* (viii:19) "wherever"; e.g. *tumamang ka i-rua=mau, m-i-rung ĭau-an* "wherever I go, you will sit with me" |
| `tumang` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "where?" | *tu* (locative) + *mang*. *pa-i-alak-aw lava tumang ta ti Jesus* (v4-loc-give.birth-sj.uo perhaps where nom pa Jesus) "where will this Jesus be born?" (ii:4) |
| `ua-mang` | interrogative | **Q-WORD** | "wherefore, to what end?" | Purposive question word. *ua-mang* (xxvi:50) "wherefore?"; used to ask the purpose of an action; e.g. *ua-mang ka i-rua=kaw hia* "why/to what end have you come here?" |
| `umamang` | indefinite pronoun | **INDEF** | "to do anything" | Purposive indefinite. *umamang* (xxvii:19) "to do anything"; e.g. in contexts where no specific action is named |
| `xnĭn` | temporal adverb | **TENSE** | "now" | The only dedicated temporal adverb for present time. *kĭt'-ey, ni-ĭlingix-n=umi xnĭn ta ba-varŭx tĭn* (see-sj.uo pst-hear-uo=2p.gen now nom rdp-blasphemy 3s.gen) "look, now you have heard his blasphemies" (xxvi:65) |

## Category key

| Marker | Domain | Meaning |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **TENSE** | Verbal | Tense and temporal location |
| **MOOD** | Verbal | Mood and modality (imperative, epistemic, deontic, assertive) |
| **COND** | Verbal | Conditional, temporal, and concessive conjunctions |
| **VOICE** | Verbal | Case-linked markers tied to the voice system (nominative, default) |
| **CASE** | Nominal | Case-marking prepositions and particles |
| **DISC** | Discourse | Negation, polarity, linkage, adversativity |
| **DEIXIS** | Nominal | Proximal/distal deictic words and locative adverbs |
| **QUANT** | Nominal | Quantifiers (universal, indefinite, definite, degree) |
| **Q-WORD** | Interrogative | Question words |
| **INDEF** | Pronominal | Indefinite pronouns |
