# Siraya affixes — complete alphabetical reference

All affixes from Adelaar (2011), sorted by first character of the affix form. Type key: **PRE** prefix · **SUF** suffix · **INF** infix / reduplication · **CIR** circumfix · **CLI** clitic.

Category markers: **VOICE** · **VERB-CLASS** · **TENSE** · **ASPECT** · **MOOD** · **CAUS** · **INCH** · **CASE** · **NUMBER** · **NOML** · **ORIENT** · **INV** · **TRANSF** · **ADD** · **ADV**

---

| Affix | Type | Category | Grammatical function | Examples |
|-------|------|----------|---------------------|---------|
| `a-` / `ä-` | PRE | **ORIENT** | Comitative orientation: "with, take along, together" | `a-para` "take along"; `a-kua` "obey" |
| `aäw-` / `ääw-` | PRE | **INV** | Inversive: "undo, reverse, un-" | `m-aäw-ka-kirix` "to untie"; `m-aäw-dŭma` "to turn around" |
| `-a` | SUF | **MOOD** | Subjunctive, actor-oriented (sj) | `l‹m›ikux-a` "may he return"; `m-araraw-a` "shall see" |
| `-al=` | SUF | **MOOD** | Subjunctive actor-oriented before clitics `=ato`/`=ăpa`; allomorph of `-a` | `i-rua-al=ăpa-ko` "I'll also come"; `ătaral-al=ato` |
| `-an` | SUF | **VOICE** · **NUMBER** · **NOML** | (1) Undergoer-oriented indicative, locative/non-core highlight; (2) collective noun suffix; (3) deverbal locative noun | (1) `ka-tälax-an`; (2) `kaäwlung-an` "humanity"; (3) `ta-tulu-an` "furnace" |
| `-aney` | SUF | **MOOD** · **VOICE** | Subjunctive + undergoer (sj.uo), non-core/locative highlight | `pää-tunun-aney` "shall be delivered" |
| `-aneyl=` | SUF | **MOOD** · **VOICE** | Allomorph of `-aney` before clitics `=ato`/`=ăpa` | `pää-tunun-aneyl=ato` |
| `-aw` | SUF | **MOOD** · **VOICE** | Subjunctive + undergoer (sj.uo), lexically restricted set | `kan-aw` "let it be eaten"; `ătaral-aw` |
| `-awl=` | SUF | **MOOD** · **VOICE** | Allomorph of `-aw` before clitics `=ato`/`=ăpa` | `kan-awl=ato` |
| `-än` | SUF | **CASE** | Oblique case suffix on human nouns | `tini-än` "him/her (obl)"; `ĭmian-än` "us (obl, excl)" |
| `Ca-` | INF | **ASPECT** · **NOML** | (1) Progressive aspect / stative on verbs; (2) deverbal noun (agent, instrument, undergoer, abstract) | (1) `k‹m›a-kading` "watching (ongoing)"; (2) `da-dĭlux` "priest" |
| `Ca- … -an` | CIR | **NOML** · **ORIENT** | Deverbal locative noun: "place where X happens" (locative reading only when both Ca- and -an co-occur) | `ta-tulu-an` "furnace/oven"; `vato-to-an` "stony place" |
| `-ey` | SUF | **MOOD** · **VOICE** | Subjunctive + undergoer (sj.uo); dominant/default form | `ătaral-ey` "may it be forgiven"; `kĭta-ey` "see it!" |
| `-eyl=` | SUF | **MOOD** · **VOICE** | Allomorph of `-ey` before clitics `=ato`/`=ăpa` | `ătaral-eyl=ato` |
| `i-` / `ĭ-` | PRE | **ORIENT** | Location orientation: "in space or time" | `m-i-rung` "to sit"; `i-rua` "to arrive" |
| `i- … -an` / `-ǝn` | CIR | **NOML** · **ORIENT** | Deverbal locative noun: "place/time/act of X" | `i-ka-kua-ǝn` "room"; `i-da-rinux-an` "eternity, world" |
| `-i` | SUF | **MOOD** | Imperative suffix (actor or undergoer) | `ad-i-ko` "bring them to me!" |
| `-ing` | SUF | **VERB-CLASS** | Verbalises ailment/body-part nouns; variant of `-ǝn` in Utrecht MS dialect | `aĭngĭt-ing` "to have a fever" |
| `ka-` | PRE | **VERB-CLASS** | (1) Class 1 verb marker (stative/property verbs); (2) appears in causative `pa-ka-` and nominal circumfix `ka-…-an` | (1) `ka-riang` "goodness"; (2) `pa-ka-kuptix` "to purify" |
| `ka- … -an` | CIR | **NOML** · **NUMBER** | (1) Abstract/stative noun from class 1 verb; (2) inclusive totality on nouns: "all of X, entire X" | (1) `ka-patey-an` "death"; `ka-takut-an` "fear"; (2) `ka-tălax-an` "entire household" |
| `ka- … -ǝn` | CIR | **NOML** | Abstract noun from class 1 verb: "quality/state of X" | `ka-muy-ǝn` "desire"; `ka-ilx-ǝn` "strength"; `ka-kan-ǝn` "food" |
| `käwx-` | PRE | **INCH** | Inchoative: "become X" (class 1 verbs and nouns) | `käwx-darikax` "to recover"; `käwx-vato` "to turn to stone" |
| `‹m›` | INF | **VOICE** · **VERB-CLASS** | Actor voice, class 3 verbs; infixed after initial consonant | `k‹m›ĭta` "to see"; `d‹m›ingding` "to judge"; `l‹m›ikux` "to return" |
| `m-` | PRE | **VOICE** | Actor voice marker (most verb classes) | `m-u-rbo` "to go inside"; `m-i-rung` "to sit" |
| `ma-` | PRE | **VOICE** · **VERB-CLASS** | Actor voice, class 1 (stative) and class 4 verbs | `ma-patey` "to die"; `ma-riang` "to be good/great" |
| `măka-` | PRE | **CASE** · **ORIENT** | Ablative preposition/prefix: "from, since, for" | `măka-rbo` "from inside"; `măka-saat` "since one (day)" |
| `mi-` | PRE | **INCH** | Inchoative: "become X" (class 1 verbs) | `mi-vulas` "to become sad"; `mi-variou` "to replace" |
| `-n` | SUF | **VOICE** | Undergoer-oriented indicative; allomorph of `-ǝn` after vowel-final roots | `kĭta-n` "it is seen"; `ka-vana-n` "speech (uo)" |
| `ni-` | PRE | **TENSE** | Past tense (pst) | `ni-k‹m›ĭta` "he saw"; `ni-pa-u-pänäx` "was shown" |
| `p-` | PRE | **CAUS** | Shortened causative `pa-` before orientation prefixes `u-`/`äw-` | `p-u-pänäx` "to show"; `p-u-rbo` "to cause to go in" |
| `pa-` | PRE | **CAUS** · **VERB-CLASS** | (1) Causative (general); (2) class 4 verb-forming prefix | (1) `pa-ka-patey` "to kill"; (2) `pa-i-sasu-an` "kingdom" |
| `pa- … -an` | CIR | **NOML** · **VERB-CLASS** | Deverbal noun from class 4 verb; locative or abstract meaning | `pa-kakĭtil-an` "wedding"; `pa-titil-an` "difference of opinion" |
| `pa- … -ǝn` | CIR | **NOML** · **VERB-CLASS** | Deverbal noun from class 4 verb; undergoer/result sense | `pa-ta-täutäux-ǝn` "doctrine"; `paku-su-ǝn` "commandment" |
| `pa-ka-` | PRE | **CAUS** | Causative of class 1 verbs | `pa-ka-kuptix` "to purify"; `pa-ka-riang-a` "to save" |
| `pa-pa-` | PRE | **CAUS** | Causative of class 4 verbs | `pa-pa-reya` "to make peace"; `pa-pa-kulamux` "to clothe" |
| `paha-` | PRE | **TRANSF** | Transformative: "turn X into Y" | `paha-pa-paul` "turn into bread"; `paha-viri-viri` "to translate" |
| `tama-` | PRE | **NOML** | Agentive nominalisation: "person who Vs" | `tama-dĭlux` "leader"; `tama-ma-täutäux` "teacher"; `tama-xail` "witness" |
| `-u` | SUF | **MOOD** | Imperative suffix | `kading-u=kame` "save us!" |
| `u-` / `äw-` | PRE | **ORIENT** | Motion orientation: "toward, into" | `m-u-rbo` "to go inside"; `m-äw-äwma` "go to the city" |
| `u- … -an` | CIR | **NOML** · **ORIENT** | Deverbal noun with motion orientation; result/event of motion | `u-va-va-tux-an` "celebration"; `u-va-var'x-an` "reward" |
| `-ǝn` | SUF | **VOICE** · **NOML** · **VERB-CLASS** | (1) Undergoer-oriented indicative (default); (2) deverbal noun (undergoer/abstract); (3) verbalises ailment nouns | (1) `ătaral-ǝn` "forgiven"; (2) `ka-muy-ǝn` "desire"; (3) `dĭmi-dĭmi-ǝn` "be paralysed" |
| `=ăpa` / `='pa` | CLI | **ADD** | Additive: "and, also, too"; with `ăsi` = "not even / not yet" | `i-rua-al=ăpa-ko` "I'll also come"; `ăsi=ăpa` "not even" |
| `=ato` / `='to` | CLI | **ASPECT** | Perfective: completed or expected event | `ma-takut=ato` "became afraid"; `kidi=ato` "it must (happen)" |
| `=da` / `=ra` | CLI | **ADV** | Adversative: "but, however, contrast; new information" | `ma-lix=da` "which was strong"; `ausi=da` "but not" |

---

**Category key**

| Marker | Domain | Meaning |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **VOICE** | Verbal | Actor-oriented vs. undergoer-oriented |
| **VERB-CLASS** | Verbal | Class 1, 2, 3, or 4 membership |
| **TENSE** | Verbal | Past vs. non-past |
| **ASPECT** | Verbal | Perfective, progressive |
| **MOOD** | Verbal | Subjunctive, imperative |
| **CAUS** | Verbal | Causative derivation |
| **INCH** | Verbal | Inchoative "become X" |
| **CASE** | Nominal | Nominative, oblique, ablative |
| **NUMBER** | Nominal | Collective, inclusive totality |
| **NOML** | Derivational | Nominalisation of any kind |
| **ORIENT** | Derivational | Motion, location, comitative orientation |
| **INV** | Derivational | Inversive "undo/reverse" |
| **TRANSF** | Derivational | Transformative "turn X into Y" |
| **ADD** | Discourse | Additivity "also, too" |
| **ADV** | Discourse | Adversativity "but, however" |

---

**Notes**

1. Pronominal clitics (`=ko`, `=kaw`, `=kame`, `=kamu`, `=kĭta`, `=(m)au`, `=(m)hu`, etc.) are not included above as they are a closed paradigm listed separately in the pronoun table.
2. `măka-` straddles the boundary between prefix and free preposition; Adelaar treats it as usually attaching to the following word.
3. Several entries carry two or three category markers (e.g. `-an` is simultaneously **VOICE** + **NUMBER** + **NOML**); this reflects genuine polyfunctionality of the surface form, not ambiguity in analysis.
4. Allomorphs (`-al=`, `-eyl=`, `-awl=`, `-aneyl=`) share the category of their base form and are not separately listed in the key.
